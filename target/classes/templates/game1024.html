Game Rules

Welcome to Game 1024! Here's how to play:

  • Use your arrow keys to move the tiles.
  • When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!
  • The objective is to create a tile with the number 1024.
  • If you reach 1024, you can continue playing to achieve even higher scores.
  • The game ends when the board is full and no more moves are possible.
